# Mason's Lab Notebook

Every real engineer keeps a lab notebook. This is yours. Log your builds, experiments, and ideas here.

---

## How to Use This Notebook

After you build something cool, add an entry below. A good lab entry has:

1. **What I built** — one sentence
2. **What I learned** — the coolest thing you discovered
3. **What I'd change** — how you'd improve it next time
4. **Difficulty** — Easy / Medium / Hard / Boss Level
